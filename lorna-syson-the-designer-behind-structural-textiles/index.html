structural textiles by Lorna Syson

Lorna Syson, the designer behind Structural Textiles

creates three dimensional textile installations for interior spaces. Her high quality surface solutions are used for acoustic panels, wall hangings, space dividers and screens.

Lorna will be exhibiting a number of pieces including her Bermondsey wall-hanging made from 100% wool, a section of her wool felt veil from her commission at the West London Buddhist centre and a compact version of her latest textile light commissioned for a large, three story entrance space in San Francisco.

All of Lorna’s structural textile designs are handmade and have been tailored to the individual projects from conception to installation. Each unique piece enhances its environment by adding warmth, texture and colour as well as reducing noise.

Lorna's bespoke design service walks your through the commissioning process to help you make clear decisions for your projects. Her innovative designs strive to be eco-concious and sustainable, where possible using 100% wool felt and recycled materials.
